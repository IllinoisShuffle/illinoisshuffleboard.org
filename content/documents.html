The Illinois Shuffleboard Association is a Not-For-Profit Corporation formed in the state of Illinois. To request a copy of our 1023 Application Form, email contact@illinoisshuffle.org. Our filed Business Income Tax Returns (Form 990-T) will also be available after our first year's return.
