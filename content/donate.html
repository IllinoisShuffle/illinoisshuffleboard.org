Support ILSA

Make A Donation

Donations support our mission of promoting interest and play in the game of shuffleboard, across the state of Illinois and beyond

Donate with Zelle

Zelle allows you to donate to ILSA without any fees, if your bank offers it.
Simply specify donate@illinoisshuffleboard.org as the Recipient.

Donate by Credit Card

We also accept donations via Visa, Mastercard or Discover credit cards.
Donate by Credit Card
Please avoid American Express cards, as the fees are high
