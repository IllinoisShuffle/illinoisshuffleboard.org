Join ILSA

Become A Member Today!

Membership costs $25 per year, and benefits include:

Our Singles Leagues

If you're in Chicago and looking for a little more shuffleboard competition, we host members-only singles leagues that coincide with Royal Palms Chicago's Team Leagues.

Tournaments

Compete in our State Tournaments, along with National Tournaments hosted around the country. We're excited to welcome the rest of the country to Illinois for our first National Tournament August 6th-7th, 2022!

Exclusive Swag

Our first 50 members will receive a Founder's pin as a thank you for joining! Other members-only apparel will be available in the future.

Supporting Shuffleboard

Becoming a member will help enable us to promote the sport of shuffleboard across the state! We plan to spend time and resources to help more people love this sport.

The Illinois Shuffleboard Association is a Not-For-Profit Corporation formed in the state of Illinois. We have filed for federal tax-exempt status under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, and our application is pending. If approved, donations will be tax deductible.
