Welcome to the Illinois Shuffleboard Association (ILSA)

It's National Tournament Weekend!

We're excited to host our first National Shuffleboard Tournament this weekend, August 6th and 7th! 64 Teams from nine states will be competing for a total of $4,000 in prizes - $1,000 going to the champions.

All of the action is going down at Royal Palms Chicago, 1750 N Milwaukee Ave, Chicago, IL. Spectators 21+ are welcome to attend both days, but Sunday at 1pm are when the best matches will begin.
